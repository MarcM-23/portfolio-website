Restaurant Database using MySQL and Java

Created a restaurant database that can provide users with information on Employees, Customers, Menu items, and Order information. The employee table stores information about the restaurant's employees, including their unique employee ID, name, and job position at the restaurant. This table allows the restaurant to efficiently organize its staff and assign roles based on the customers experience.

The Customer table stores information about the restaurant's customers. It also includes a unique ID for each customer, the customer's name, and their phone number. This helps the restaurant manage customers and provides ways to find and contact them. The Menu table stores information about the list of items for sale in the restaurant.

Each menu item has a unique item ID, name, and price. This table helps manage items on the menu and allows the restaurant to easily update and modify them. The Orders table stores information on individual orders, including unique order IDs, customer IDs, employee IDs of staff serving, and timestamps showing when orders were placed.
